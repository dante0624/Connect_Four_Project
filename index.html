How it Works

In this game, red always plays first and yellow always plays second. Below the board, it says which color will win and in how many moves, assuming perfect play from both players. The numbers above each column indicates who will win if the current player places their chip in that slot.

A positive number indicates that red will win, a negative number indicates that yellow will win, and 0 indicates a draw. So +5 means that red will win in five turns, -4 means that yellow will win in four turns, and 0 is a draw (which always happens when the board is full).
